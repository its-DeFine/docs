## AUDIENCE DEFINITION

The Community tab is accessed by **all six human types** in the Livepeer ecosystem. However, the primary sustained audience is the **Network Participant Seeking Connection** — someone already invested (emotionally, financially, or technically) in the ecosystem and now seeking the human layer.

### Secondary Audiences (lighter use)

| Audience | Why they arrive | What they need | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Curious Newcomer** | Social link, homepage referral, landing page | Orientation + entry point | 5 min |
| **Governance Participant** | Link from Forum/proposal | Process explainer, surfaces, GovWorks | 10 min |
| **Active Delegator** | Looking for voting, staying current | Governance surfaces, community channels | 10 min |
| **Potential Contributor** | Discord or referral | Contribution taxonomy, funded pathways | 15 min |
| **External Evaluator** | Research phase | Community health signals | 20 min |

## THE FIVE DISTINCT JOURNEYS

### JOURNEY A: Find the Community

**Persona:** Network Participant with Intent A  
**Arriving question:** "Where do I talk to people about Livepeer?"  
**Leaving with:** Joined the right platform or channel; confidence there is an active community  

| Step | Reader's question | What they need | Content vehicle |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Where do I talk to people? | Platform map with purpose per surface | Channels page |
| 2 | Which platform is right for my question? | Discord vs Forum vs Telegram explained | Channels page (decision table) |
| 3 | In Discord, where do I go for my role? | Channel guide by role | Discord Channel Guide section |
| 4 | How do I introduce myself? | Community norms + #introductions link | Community Guidelines + Channels |
| 5 | Is anyone actually here? | Evidence of activity | Calendar, event links, #general channel |
| 6 | What do I do if I get stuck? | Escalation: support channels, GovWorks | Channels page (support row) |

**Success metric:** Visitor joins Discord, finds #landing-pad or role-specific channel, and introduces self.

**Content dependencies:**
- Channels page (complete)
- Community Guidelines (complete)
- Luma calendar link (live source)

---

### JOURNEY B: Follow What's Happening

**Persona:** Network Participant with Intent B; Active Delegator; Governance Participant  
**Arriving question:** "What's the latest? What are people discussing? What's happening in governance?"  
**Leaving with:** Subscribed to one or more active information sources; knows where proposals live  

| Step | Reader's question | What they need | Content vehicle |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | What are people discussing right now? | Forum governance category link + latest threads | Governance Participation page |
| 2 | Are there active proposals to vote on? | Explorer link; Forum proposal threads | Governance Participation page |
| 3 | What are the recurring calls? When are they? | Call schedule + calendar link | Events and Calls page |
| 4 | Where does Livepeer publish? | Blog, X, YouTube, Forum, Live Pioneers recap | News and Updates page |
| 5 | How do I stay current without checking everywhere? | Subscription model per source | News and Updates page |
| 6 | Where is the technical roadmap? | GitHub releases, Forum LIP status | News and Updates page (link to LIP repo) |

**Success metric:** Visitor has bookmarked Forum governance category, subscribed to one news source, and found next recurring call.

**Content dependencies:**
- Governance Participation page (complete, links to Explorer)
- Events and Calls page (complete)
- News and Updates page (complete)
- Forum governance category (live source)
- Luma calendar (live source)

### JOURNEY C: Contribute

**Persona:** Potential Contributor; Network Participant with Intent C  
**Arriving question:** "I want to do more than [operate/hold/build]. How do I contribute?"  
**Leaving with:** Identified one contribution type matching their skills; found funded pathway or entry point  

| Step | Reader's question | What they need | Content vehicle |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | What types of contribution exist? | Taxonomy of six types: code, docs, governance, support, translation, ideas | Ways to Contribute page |
| 2 | What's a realistic first action for [my type]? | Concrete first step per type (e.g., "open a GitHub issue," "post in Forum") | Ways to Contribute page (first action row) |
| 3 | Is there funding available? | Funded vs non-funded pathways by type | Opportunities page |
| 4 | What funded programs exist? | Foundation RFPs, SPEs, workstream roles, grants | Opportunities page (table) |
| 5 | How do I actually join a workstream? | Workstream lead contact, Forum link | Workstreams page |
| 6 | What if I want to contribute code? | Link to Developers tab (grants, RFPs, bounties) | Ways to Contribute → For Builders CTA |
| 7 | How do I get recognised? | Community tools, GitHub, GovWorks proposal credit | Community Guides (recognition mechanisms) |

**Success metric:** Visitor has identified one contribution type, knows the first action, and has either started that action or found the relevant team contact.

**Content dependencies:**
- Ways to Contribute page (complete)
- Opportunities page (complete; links to live RFP source)
- Workstreams page (complete; links to Forum threads)
- Community Guides (recognition section)
- Cross-link to Developers tab

### JOURNEY D: Understand Governance

**Persona:** Governance Participant; Delegator; Newcomer  
**Arriving question:** "How does governance work? How do I participate? How do I propose something?"  
**Leaving with:** Understands four participation levels; knows where proposals live; knows GovWorks contact  

| Step | Reader's question | What they need | Content vehicle |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | What can I actually do in governance? | Four levels: read, comment, vote, propose | Governance Participation page |
| 2 | How do I vote? | Link to Explorer; reference to voting mechanics in LPT tab | Governance Participation page |
| 3 | How do I comment on a proposal? | Forum governance category; how to comment | Governance Participation page |
| 4 | How do I propose something? | SPE process (5-phase) + GovWorks contact | Governance Participation page + FAQ |
| 5 | What are SPEs and workstreams? | Definitions + list of active workstreams | FAQ + Workstreams page |
| 6 | Who is GovWorks and how do they help? | Description, Notion Hub link, contact info | Governance Participation page (GovWorks section) |
| 7 | For full governance mechanics, where? | Cross-link to LPT tab → Governance | Governance Participation page (Go Deeper) |

**Success metric:** Visitor can name the four participation levels, knows where to find active proposals, and has GovWorks link bookmarked.

**Content dependencies:**
- Governance Participation page (complete)
- Workstreams page (complete)
- FAQ page (SPE/workstream definitions)
- Cross-link to LPT tab → Governance section

### JOURNEY E: Evaluate Community Health (Researcher / Evaluator)

**Persona:** External evaluator, researcher, press  
**Arriving question:** "Is this community real and active?"  
**Leaving with:** Data points about community size, governance maturity, and activity level  

| Step | Reader's question | What they need | Content vehicle |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Who is in this community? | Size signal, role breakdown | Who's Here page; Portal |
| 2 | How is it organised? | Foundation + SPEs + workstreams structure | Who's Here page (Foundation section) |
| 3 | Is there active governance? | Proposal frequency, voting participation | Governance Participation page (links to Explorer) |
| 4 | What's the event frequency? | Recurring calls, conferences, hackathons | Events and Calls page |
| 5 | How much is the community funded? | Treasury size, active SPE list | Explorer link + Community FAQ |
| 6 | What products exist? | Who's using Livepeer | [Link to Home/Products] |

**Success metric:** Evaluator has formed credible picture of community maturity and can cite specific data points.

**Content dependencies:**
- Who's Here page (complete)
- Governance Participation page (links to Explorer)
- Events and Calls page (complete)
- FAQ (treasury definitions)
- Cross-link to Home (products)
- Cross-link to About (governance model detail)
