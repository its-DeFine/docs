## Livepeer Evolution
Livepeer has evolved from a live video streaming network to a general-purpose real-time media compute network

## Livepeer Protocol and Network
The Livepeer Protocol is a set of smart contracts that implement the economic incentives and rules of the system, while the Livepeer Network is a global pool of independently operated nodes that perform the compute work of transcoding and AI inference, coordinated by the protocol and exposed through a growing ecosystem of platforms and applications.

## Livepeer Capabilities
AI & Video transcoding workloads, with a focus on low-latency, real-time applications such as live streaming and interactive AI.

## Livepeer Participants and Roles
The Livepeer Protocol defines 3 main actor roles with distinct responsibilities and incentives:
- Orchestrators - GPU nodes that perform transcoding and AI inference work, earn fees and staking rewards, and participate in governance
- Gateways - aka Broadcasters, the entities that submit video and AI jobs to the network, route work, manage payment flows, and connect to Orchestrators
- Delegators - LPT token holders who stake to an Orchestrator to secure the network and
earn rewards, and participate in governance and voting
