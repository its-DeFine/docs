

This page defines the key actors of the Livepeer protocol.

It serves as a high-level overview and introduction to the different roles and responsibilities of the participants in the Livepeer protocol, and how they interact with each other and the protocol.

It provides a mental model for understanding the different actors in the system, their incentives, and how they contribute to the overall functioning of the Livepeer network.

There are only a few core actors in the Livepeer protocol
- Orchestrators
- Gateways: aka Broadcasters, the entities that submit video and AI jobs to the network
- Transcoders / AI Workers: the entities that perform the actual compute work of transcoding and AI inference
- Verifiers: the entities that verify the correctness of the work performed by the transcoders (BUT NOT AI workers), and ensure the integrity of the network
- Delegators (?): secure the network and earn rewards by bonding their stake
