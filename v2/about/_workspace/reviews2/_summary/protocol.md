## Cross-folder duplicates flagged

**x-prefix files (5)**: All x-design, x-design-philosophy, x-livepeer-token, x-overview, x-treasury are alternates/older versions of the canonical pages (design, livepeer-token, governance-and-treasury). Only x-design is currently registered in docs.json (line 2180). x-design-philosophy is a stub draft. x-livepeer-token and x-treasury contain unique content (token utility/inflation, treasury detail) NOT in the canonical replacements - canonical merge needed before deletion.

**protocol2/ siblings (12 files)**: Every file in `v2/about/protocol/` has a near-equivalent in `v2/about/protocol2/` (different filenames in some cases: design-philosophy ↔ x-design-philosophy, technical-architecture ↔ architecture, governance-model ↔ governance-and-treasury, livepeer-token-economics ↔ livepeer-token, overview ↔ x-overview, treasury ↔ x-treasury). protocol2 also has unique files (capabilities, core-mechanisms, economics) not in protocol/. Folder split decision required.

**concepts/ duplicates**: `concepts/governance-and-economics.mdx` overlaps governance-and-treasury.mdx and x-treasury.mdx. `concepts/actors-and-participants.mdx` overlaps actors.mdx. `concepts/livepeer-stack.mdx` overlaps architecture.mdx.

**Factual conflict**: Treasury inflation cut is variously stated as 10% (governance-and-treasury.mdx, x-design.mdx) and 25% (x-treasury.mdx). Verify against on-chain `treasuryRewardCutRate` and resolve.
