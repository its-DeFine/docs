This page describes the economic model & fee flows of the network, extending on the protocol items.

How actors are incentivised to participate in the network and how value flows between them, even without clear protocol incentives.

The use of the treasury to fund ecosystem growth and development, and how it interacts with the network's economic model.

ie. how gateways set prices, how clients pay for jobs, how orchestrators earn revenue, and how the protocol's economic mechanisms (staking, rewards, slashing) interact with the marketplace dynamics to create a sustainable ecosystem for decentralized video and AI compute.
