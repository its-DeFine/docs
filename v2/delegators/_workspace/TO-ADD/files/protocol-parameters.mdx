All parameters on this page are governance-controlled. They can be changed by an approved Livepeer Improvement Proposal without a code deployment. **Never rely on values cached in secondary sources** — verify against the primary sources linked for each parameter.

---

## How to verify parameters on-chain

All parameters are readable from the deployed contracts on Arbitrum One via [Arbiscan](https://arbiscan.io/accounts/label/livepeer). The canonical address list is in [governor-scripts/updates/addresses.js](https://github.com/livepeer/governor-scripts/blob/master/updates/addresses.js) — cross-check against Arbiscan to confirm addresses are live.

For each parameter below, the contract and function name are provided for direct on-chain verification.

---

## Staking and delegation parameters

### Active set size

**What it controls:** The maximum number of orchestrators eligible to earn inflation rewards and receive routed jobs each round.

**Protocol source:** `BondingManager.numActiveTranscoders()`

**Historical value:** 100 (has been 100 since the Streamflow upgrade in 2020; governance has not changed it since)

**Why it matters to delegators:** Your orchestrator must rank within the active set by total bonded stake to earn anything. Delegating to an orchestrator outside the top N means your stake contributes to their potential activation but earns no rewards until they enter.

**Verify live:** [BondingManager on Arbiscan](https://arbiscan.io/accounts/label/livepeer) → read contract → `numActiveTranscoders`

---

### Unbonding period (thawing period)

**What it controls:** The number of rounds you must wait after calling `unbond()` before your LPT becomes withdrawable.

**Protocol source:** `BondingManager.unbondingPeriod()`

**Historical value:** 7 rounds (approximately 7 days at one round per day on Arbitrum One)

**Why it matters to delegators:** During the thawing period, your LPT earns no rewards and cannot be withdrawn. You can cancel and rebond during this window. Plan exits around this delay.

**Verify live:** [BondingManager on Arbiscan](https://arbiscan.io/accounts/label/livepeer) → read contract → `unbondingPeriod`

## Inflation parameters

### Target bonding rate

**What it controls:** The bonding participation target the inflation mechanism adjusts toward. If actual bonding is below this rate, inflation increases each round; if above, it decreases.

**Protocol source:** `BondingManager.targetBondingRate()`

**Historical value:** 50% (0.5 as a fraction)

**Why it matters to delegators:** Sets the equilibrium point of the inflation model. Inflation rates are highest when participation is furthest below 50%.

**Verify live:** [BondingManager on Arbiscan](https://arbiscan.io/accounts/label/livepeer) → read contract → `targetBondingRate`

---

### Inflation adjustment rate (alpha)

**What it controls:** The fixed per-round rate by which the inflation rate increases or decreases, depending on whether bonding is below or above target.

**Protocol source:** `BondingManager.inflationChange()`

**Historical value:** 0.00005 (0.00005% per round)

**Why it matters to delegators:** Determines how quickly inflation converges toward equilibrium. At this rate, changes accumulate slowly — a material shift in inflation rate requires many rounds.

**Verify live:** [BondingManager on Arbiscan](https://arbiscan.io/accounts/label/livepeer) → read contract → `inflationChange`

---

### Current inflation rate

**What it controls:** The per-round percentage of total LPT supply currently being minted.

**Protocol source:** `Minter.inflation()`

**Value:** Changes every round — do not use any static figure.

**Verify live:** [Livepeer Explorer](https://explorer.livepeer.org) → Protocol stats, or [Minter on Arbiscan](https://arbiscan.io/accounts/label/livepeer) → read contract → `inflation`

---

## Treasury parameters

### Treasury Reward Cut Rate

**What it controls:** The percentage of each round's inflation issuance diverted to the Community Treasury before distribution to orchestrators and delegators.

**Protocol source:** `BondingManager.treasuryRewardCutRate()`

**Governance basis:** LIP-92

**Historical value:** 10% (0.1 as a fraction)

**Why it matters to delegators:** This deduction applies before your rewards are calculated. A 10% treasury cut means 90% of each round's issuance flows to participants.

**Verify live:** [BondingManager on Arbiscan](https://arbiscan.io/accounts/label/livepeer) → read contract → `treasuryRewardCutRate`; cross-check with [LIP-92](https://github.com/livepeer/LIPs)

## Governance parameters

### Proposer bond minimum

**What it controls:** The minimum bonded LPT balance required to submit a formal on-chain governance proposal.

**Protocol source:** `LivepeerGovernor.proposalThreshold()`

**Historical value:** 100 LPT bonded (not locked separately — the proposer needs 100 LPT bonded at time of submission)

**Why it matters:** The Proposer Bond deters spam proposals by requiring skin in the game. It does not lock additional tokens.

**Verify live:** [LivepeerGovernor on Arbiscan](https://arbiscan.io/accounts/label/livepeer) → read contract → `proposalThreshold`

---

### Quorum threshold

**What it controls:** The minimum percentage of total participating bonded stake required for a governance vote to produce a binding outcome.

**Protocol source:** `LivepeerGovernor.quorumNumerator()` / `quorumDenominator()`

**Governance basis:** Set in LivepeerGovernor deployment; changeable via governance

**Verify live:** [LivepeerGovernor on Arbiscan](https://arbiscan.io/accounts/label/livepeer) → read contract → quorum functions

---

### Voting period

**What it controls:** Duration (in blocks) of the on-chain voting window for proposals.

**Protocol source:** `LivepeerGovernor.votingPeriod()`

**Verify live:** [LivepeerGovernor on Arbiscan](https://arbiscan.io/accounts/label/livepeer) → read contract → `votingPeriod`

---

### Timelock delay

**What it controls:** Mandatory delay between a proposal passing and its execution on-chain.

**Protocol source:** `TimelockController.getMinDelay()`

**Verify live:** [TimelockController on Arbiscan](https://arbiscan.io/accounts/label/livepeer) → read contract → `getMinDelay`

## Governance change history

Material parameter changes are enacted through approved LIPs. The canonical record is the [LIPs repository](https://github.com/livepeer/LIPs). Key LIPs affecting delegator parameters:

| LIP | What changed |
|---|---|
| LIP-89 | Introduced LivepeerGovernor on-chain governance, stake-weighted voting, vote detachment |
| LIP-91 | Established the Community Treasury |
| LIP-92 | Set the Treasury Reward Cut Rate |

For any other parameter change, search the LIPs repo by parameter name.
