## Quick facts

<BorderedBox variant="accent" padding="16px">

**What you get:** A proportional share of LPT inflation rewards and ETH fee revenue — both determined by your orchestrator's commission settings and their reward call reliability.

**What you commit to:** LPT bonded to one orchestrator at a time. Exiting requires an unbonding period before tokens become withdrawable. See [Protocol Parameters](/v2/delegators/resources/reference/protocol-parameters) for the current duration.

**What you keep:** Custody of your tokens at all times (BondingManager holds them, not the orchestrator). Governance voting rights on all bonded stake.

**The real risk:** Missed `Reward()` calls by your orchestrator — not slashing. Slashing is currently disabled on Livepeer mainnet. A missed reward call means zero income for that round across the entire pool.

</BorderedBox>

## Governance and Treasury

Bonded LPT earns governance weight equal to your stake. You can vote on Livepeer Improvement Proposals, override your orchestrator's vote on any proposal (vote detachment), and submit proposals if your bonded balance meets the minimum requirement.
