## What Is Livepeer?

Livepeer is the open video compute layer for the AI era.
It powers apps that stream, transform, and remix video — all in real time.

Built on a decentralized network of GPUs, Livepeer lets developers and creators run high-performance video and AI workflows without relying on expensive cloud infrastructure.##

From individuals to enterprises, and from creators to innovators and entrepeneurs, Livepeer caters to anyone looking for a customisable, dynamically scalable, cost effective, on-demand real-time AI video platform for their business, side project or creative pursuit:

- Developers & Founders — ship products with video + AI faster, cheaper, and openly
- Creators & Performers — transform live video into art with no setup
- Orchestrators & Infra People — earn by running jobs for the network

## What Makes Livepeer Different?

- 🧱 Fully programmable, composable video infrastructure
- 🌍 Runs on a global, open GPU network — not cloud monopolies
- 💰 Pay-as-you-go, usage-based pricing — no over-provisioning
- 🔓 Open source, developer-first, no gatekeeping

Livepeer offers a decentralised, open-source platform for video streaming and AI that dramatically reduces costs by utilising idle compute resources.

It provides:

- **Transparency** via blockchain verification
- **Scalability** for high-demand workloads
- **AI integration** and modular machine-learning pipelines
- **Developer tools** for fast innovation
- **Earnings** for GPU operators and LPT holders

By empowering a distributed ecosystem, Livepeer disrupts traditional centralised streaming platforms.
