### Why Livepeer?

Livepeer offers a decentralised, open-source platform for video streaming, providing significant cost benefits by leveraging idle computing resources. Its innovative use of blockchain technology ensures transparency and security, while its scalability allows users to efficiently handle high-demand streaming needs. Additionally, Livepeer's integration capabilities with AI and other technologies make it adaptable for various applications, providing developers with a robust toolset. By empowering token holders and orchestrators, Livepeer fosters a community-driven ecosystem, differentiating itself from traditional centralised streaming solutions.
