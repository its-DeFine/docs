## How to review

Each part is a separate file. Review them in order. For each one:

- **APPROVE** — it's correct, move on
- **AMEND** — mostly right, note what needs changing
- **REJECT** — fundamentally wrong, needs rethinking

Mark your verdict at the top of each file.

## After review

Once all parts are reviewed, decisions get recorded and we can either:
- **Lock the IA** and proceed to content work (top-down)
- **Start bottom-up** on individual pages using just the approved parts (e.g. use approved terminology + voice rules, ignore section structure if it's wrong)
- **Mix** — lock what's good, redo what's not
