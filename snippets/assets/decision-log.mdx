## Executive Summary

1. The favicon bundle is no longer split across root and `site/`; `snippets/assets/logos/favicon/favicon.png` is now the single canonical runtime favicon.
2. All active `/site` OG consumers were repointed to `snippets/assets/media/og-images/`.
3. `style.css` now points at `snippets/assets/media/images/site/united-kingdom-flag-icon.svg`.
4. The stale asset copy of `sitemap-ai.xml` is gone; root `sitemap-ai.xml` remains the only canonical sitemap.
5. `domain/` remains in place until the phase-two audit is approved.
