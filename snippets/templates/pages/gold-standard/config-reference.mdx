PURPOSE: Structured lookup of flags, options, parameters.

WHEN TO USE:
- Primary on reference (specification) pages
- Sometimes on instruction pages (inline flag documentation within setup steps)
- Sometimes on guide pages (configuration options section)

WHEN NOT TO USE:
- concept, tutorial, navigation, resource pages

RULES:
- Grouped by function (not alphabetical) — reader looks by what they want to do
- Searchable when >20 entries (use SearchTable)
- Inline in procedures: maximum 5 flags (use AccordionGroup variant)
- Every entry has: name, type/value, description, default (if applicable)
*/}
