{/*
SECTION BLOCK: Decision Matrix
PURPOSE: Help reader choose between options.

WHEN TO USE:
- Primary on guide pages (choosing deployment mode, pricing strategy, etc.)
- Sometimes on concept pages (comparing types, modes, approaches)
- Sometimes on reference pages (feature comparison)

WHEN NOT TO USE:
- tutorial, instruction pages (these execute, they don't choose)
- navigation pages

RULES:
- Equal visual weight across all options (no bias in layout)
- Include recommendation or decision summary AFTER the matrix
- No "vs" in headings — use governing-concept labels
- For 2-3 simple routing choices, use CardGroup instead of a table
*/}
