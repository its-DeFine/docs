## Audit Notes

The CONTENT-WRITING plan is the most mature and well-structured of the 5 plans. Its PM infrastructure is solid: gate definitions are clear, the decision-registry protocol is enforced, and the blocking-items format is correct. The main gap is that blocking-items.md is empty despite multiple documented blockers living in project-state.md and decision-registry.md — those items should be migrated into blocking-items.md per the protocol.

The plan-canonical.md accurately captures what is done vs not done. The tab-status.md symbols are accurate for the official CONTENT-WRITING pipeline. However, the CONTENTI-PIPLEINE and ORCHESTRATOR-CONTENT-WRITING plans have advanced content for Orchestrators and Gateways independently of the CONTENT-WRITING gate sequence — these are parallel work streams that the CONTENT-WRITING plan does not reflect.

Critical path: the veracity-pass.md prompt needs test runs and formal approval before Phase 7 can run at scale. The S01-S12 pages written in CONTENTI-PIPLEINE are the immediate test candidates.
