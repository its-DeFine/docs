# IA Maps, Templates, and Site Structure

**What these are**: Completed and in-progress information architecture maps plus the output templates used to create them. Ground truth for what the current site structure actually is.
**Use in**: Phase 1 (audience design — cross-tab ownership check) and Phase 2 (structure audit — fit to locked sections).

---

## Files

### site-map.md — 9-tab ownership map
**Source**: `workspace/plan/active/CONTENT-WRITING/Prompts/Previous Prompts Used/site-map/site-map.md`
**What it contains**: Ownership map for all 9 tabs. Boundary notes (what each tab doesn't own). Duplication risk register (content that could appear in multiple tabs). The embedded site ownership map in `audience-design.md` was derived from this file.
**Use**: Load before Phase 1 to ground the cross-tab ownership gate.

### orchestrators-ia-map.md — quality reference
**Source**: `workspace/plan/active/CONTENT-WRITING/Prompts/Previous Prompts Used/derive-ia/orchestrators-ia-map.md`
**What it contains**: Complete IA map for the Orchestrators tab. Shows the quality standard for a finished Phase 1+2 output. Includes: personas A/B/C/D, JTBDs, ideal section structure, exit states (concrete), persona path validation.
**Use**: Show to AI as a quality example of what Phase 1 output should look like before producing a new tab's IA map.

### 08a-ia-per-tab.md — locked section vocabulary
**Source**: `workspace/plan/active/CONTENT-WRITING/Prompts/Previous Prompts Used/derive-ia/08a-ia-per-tab.md`
**What it contains**: Locked section vocabulary for Gateways and Orchestrators tabs. Defines the canonical section names (portal, navigator, concepts, quickstart, setup, guides, resources/reference, resources/knowledge-hub) and what each section contains.
**Use**: Load into Phase 2 (structure audit) as the constraint set for fitting existing pages to locked sections.

### TEMPLATE-tab-ia-map.md — output template
**Source**: `workspace/plan/active/CONTENT-WRITING/Prompts/Previous Prompts Used/derive-ia/TEMPLATE-tab-ia-map.md`
**What it contains**: Template for the Phase 1 IA map output (3 parts: audience analysis, JTBD table, section shape table).
**Use**: This is what Phase 1 output should look like. The `audience-design.md` prompt outputs in this format.

### community-tab-04 — cross-tab ownership example
**Source**: `workspace/plan/active/CONTENT-WRITING/Prompts/Previous Prompts Used/derive-ia/community-tab-04-context-and-recalibration.md`
**What it contains**: Shows how cross-tab ownership analysis works in practice. Community tab was overclaiming 12 content areas from other tabs. Recalibration reduced scope from 20 pages to 14. Example of the pre-Phase-2 gate in action.

### community-tab-05 — final IA example
**Source**: `workspace/plan/active/CONTENT-WRITING/Prompts/Previous Prompts Used/derive-ia/community-tab-05-final-ia-and-pages.md`
**What it contains**: Final Community tab IA after recalibration. 14 pages with full structure.
