## Purpose

Convert the Livepeer Docs repo from human-owner governance to fully ownerless, community-operable governance — where every governed surface has one canonical source, one deterministic validator, one repair path, and one primary gate layer. The repo is being handed to the **OSS community + a human review team**. No internal owner after handover.

## Master Constraint (added 2026-03-28)

This repo is an **ownerless community repo** from handover onwards. Every governance decision, automation, and document must work without internal knowledge, named maintainers, or private context. The human review team handles escalations only.
