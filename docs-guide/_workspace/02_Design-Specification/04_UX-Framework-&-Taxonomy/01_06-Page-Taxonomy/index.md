## **Page Taxonomy**

Ten page types. Each has a fixed layout pattern, target metrics, and section order.

### **Quick Reference**

| **Page Type** | **Job** | **Target Time** | **Word Range** | **Required Components** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| landing | Route the reader | &lt;10 sec | 50-150 | CardGroup, Cards |
| overview | Orient and motivate | 2-4 min | 300-800 | Prose, Cards (routing) |
| concept | Explain a mechanism | 5-10 min | 500-2000 | Prose, diagrams, Notes |
| tutorial | Activate (zero to success) | 10-30 min | 500-1500 | Steps, CodeBlock, Tabs |
| how_to | Enable a specific task | 5-15 min | 300-1000 | Steps, CodeBlock, Tabs |
| guide | Practical understanding | 5-15 min | 500-2000 | Prose, tables, Accordion |
| reference | Lookup facts | &lt;1 min/lookup | Varies | Tables, ResponseField |
| faq | Answer common questions | 1-2 min | Varies | AccordionGroup |
| troubleshooting | Fix problems by symptom | 1-5 min | Varies | AccordionGroup, CodeBlock |
| glossary | Define terms | <30 sec/term | Varies | SearchTable or list |
